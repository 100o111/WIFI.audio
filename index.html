https://wifi.audio is a sound installation and performance by Eyal Talmor and Mika Ben Amar, where mobile phones become nodes in a networked sonic field. Through a Wi-Fi network, sound is distributed, spatialized, and dynamically reshaped across devices, forming a continuously shifting composition. Blurring the boundary between audience, performer, and technological infrastructure, the project explores how collective presence transforms an ephemeral, networked soundscape.
