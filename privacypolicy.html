Privacy Policy for wifi.audio

TL;DR

Don't worry, we're not hacking your phone or spying on you. We just need your name so we know who's who during the experience. Your microphone is only used on your own device to create sounds, we never record or send that audio anywhere. Your camera is used to activate the flashlight (only if you have an Android phone), and we also don't record or send any video recording of yours. We can't access any of your files or personal data. The wifi connection just lets your phone talk to our local server during the event, and we use network activity to create audio-visual effects. So relax and enjoy the experience!

Last updated: May 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how wifi.audio ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and processes your personal data when you participate in our interactive audio experience. We are committed to protecting your privacy in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

2. Data Controller

For the purposes of GDPR, wifi.audio is the data controller responsible for your personal data. For any privacy-related questions, you may contact us at: crclrk@protonmail.com

3. Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal data:

  • Your name (provided by you when you RSVP and at the start of the experience)
  • Your email address (for RSVP and event communication only)
  • Technical connection data (device type, browser information)
  • Network activity detection (for interactive sound generation only)

4. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Manage event attendance and send event reminders
  • Identify you within the interactive experience
  • Facilitate communication between your device and our local server
  • Generate audio-visual effects based on network activity
  • Improve the interactive experience
  • Contact you about future wifi.audio performances (you can opt out anytime)

5. Device Permissions

During the performance, we may request access to:

  • Microphone: Only used on your device to create sounds. We never record or transmit audio.
  • Camera: Only used to activate the flashlight on Android devices. We never record or transmit video.

We cannot and do not access any of your files or other personal data on your device.

6. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Your explicit consent when joining the experience
  • The legitimate interest of providing the interactive audio experience

7. Data Security and Retention

  • All data processing during the performance occurs locally within our event network
  • Your microphone is accessed only on your device and is never recorded or transmitted
  • We do not access any files on your device
  • Performance data is automatically deleted when the session ends
  • RSVP information is retained only for event management and future event notifications (with your consent)

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Opt out of future communications

9. Third-Party Services

We use Formspree for RSVP form processing. Their privacy policy can be found at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please contact us at: crclrk@protonmail.com
